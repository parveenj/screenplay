Welcome my friend! My name is Parveen and I'm a design leader working in the SF Bay Area. I'm a seasoned design professional who begins every project with an open mind and fierce curiosity about people and process.

And yet, my first love growing up was creative storytelling, when I used to spend time writing novels, short stories, and screenplays. If you asked me what my dream job was when I was in high school, it would have been to write and direct motion pictures as I thought it was the most engaging story format of all time.

These days, I bring this background to every project I work on as build products that engage with it's users. This website is about my recent design work but also inspired by my pre-digital inspirations, a fusion of my past and present that makes me whole.
