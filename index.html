Welcome my friend! My name is Parveen and I'm a design leader working in the SF Bay Area.

As a child, I dreamt of making movies so that I connect myself to others. But now as an adult, I make products so that I can connect people to each other. This is my portfolio where I take inspiration from the former to display the latter.

Most of my work has been for the enterprise web, which I have done with some of the biggest names in the valley.

So what's with this movie theme? In my opinion, movies provide the best experience for a good story as it takes most of your senses and provides a full narrative in a concise package. Why not take that as proper inspiration to share my stories with you. If you don't care about the story and just want the spoilers, check this page out here.
