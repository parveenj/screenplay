Welcome my friend! My name is Parveen and I'm a design leader working in the SF Bay Area who begins every project with an open mind and fierce curiosity about people and process.

And yet, my first love growing up was creative storytelling, when I used to spend time writing novels, short stories, and screenplays. If you asked me what my dream job was when I was in high school, it would have been to write and direct motion pictures as I believed it was the most engaging story format of all time.

These days, I bring this background to every project I work on as I build products that engage with it's users and stakeholders. This website may focus on my recent design work but it also inspired by my prior inspirations, a fusion of my past and present that makes me whole.
