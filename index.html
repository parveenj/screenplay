Welcome my friend! My name is Parveen and I'm a design leader working in the SF Bay Area. I'm a seasoned design professional who begins every project with an open mind and fierce curiosity about people and process.

And yet my first love growing up was creative storytelling where I used to spend time writing novels, short stories, and screenplays. If you asked me my dream job in high school, it would have been to write and direct motion pictures as I think its the most engaging story format of all time. These days, I bring this background to every project I work on as build products that engage to it's users. My website is dedicated to the fusion of my past and present since that makes me whole.
